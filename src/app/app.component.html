About the Challenge

Connecting Generative AI with building physics research

Research-oriented

The challenge invites participants to explore how GenAI can be used to support reasoning, analysis, and interpretation in building physics.

Application-driven

The competition focuses on realistic workflows such as simulation interpretation, performance evaluation, and design decision support.

Human-AI collaboration

The goal is not only automation, but also meaningful collaboration between domain experts, students, and AI systems.
